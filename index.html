About me

I am Hanyu Xue, a student of computer science. I get my bachelor degree from East China Normal University. With great passion of computer science and wide range of knowledge in this field, I participate in the development of many interesting projects and apps.

With a two-years research experience in Digital Entertainment Laboratory, I finished some researches in graphics and computer vision. And I would like to continue to study and research in this field. During these years, I lead a team to conduct some research in 3D liquid scene construction. If you have interest in the my work, please do not hesitate to contact me.

In my spare time I like practicing handwriting and writing. I have more than ten years experience of Chinese handwriting, if you have same hobby maybe we can we can sit down, have a drink and talk about this Chinese traditional culture.
