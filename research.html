Research

About research

In the last two years, I do some researches in computer visions and graphics. These researches concern foreground extracting automatically, images blending, Augmented Reality and liquid scene construction. The most important part of the work is liquid scene construction.

Liquid scene construction is consist of two parts, liquid reconstruction and scene construction. The technology uses a common 2D fluid video as example inputs. The synthesized fluid will be lager in size and possess similar characteristic with the source videos.
